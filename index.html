01 — Who we are

Think global.
Act local.

NayePankh Foundation started the way most real change does — a handful of students in Kanpur deciding that "someone should do something" meant them. Today we're one of India's largest student-run NGOs, with chapters active in Kanpur, Ghaziabad, and beyond.

We believe service to people is service to something larger. Every drive, every donated rupee, every Saturday morning spent packing meals — it adds up to a society that looks out for itself. That's the whole model: ordinary students, organized well, moving fast.

02 — What we run

Four wings. One bird.

Each program is its own small machine — but they all fly toward the same thing.

🍛

Free Meals

Hot food drives for families and children who'd otherwise go without — run by volunteers, funded by donors, served with no questions asked.

🩷

Sanitary Pads

Breaking the silence around periods by getting sanitary products directly into the hands of girls and women who need them.

🧥

Clothes & Winter Care

Clothing drives that keep the underprivileged warm through North India's harsh winters — collected, sorted, and distributed by hand.

📚

Free Education

Tutoring and learning support for kids who don't have access to formal schooling — because a foundation isn't just food and clothes.
